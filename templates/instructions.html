Welcome to the RetinoGuard AI Survey

Understanding Your Role

This interface is designed to allow experts like you to evaluate retinal images and provide a critical assessment of our AI model's predictions, enhanced with Grad-CAM visualizations.

Instructions
  1. Initial Analysis:
    • Examine the provided retinal image (1ST Image) carefully.
    • Assign a Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) severity rating based on your expert analysis.
    • Select the appropriate DR rating from the drop-down menu.
    • Submit your rating by clicking 'Submit Rating'.
  2. Grad-CAM Visualization and Model Prediction Review:
    • After submission, you will see the AI model's prediction along with a heat map overlay (2ND Image).
    • The heat map, generated by Grad-CAM, highlights the areas in the image that most influenced the AI's prediction, providing insight into its decision-making process.
    • Review the highlighted areas and compare them against the AI's prediction and your initial analysis.
    • Evaluate the alignment of the model's focus areas with the pathological features you identify as relevant.
    • Select an accuracy score for the visual diagnosis and disease severity from the drop-down menus.
    • Provide your feedback on the AI's visual interpretation and severity assessment by clicking 'Submit Feedback'.
What to Expect Next

After submitting your ratings and feedback, you will be directed to a brief survey where you can share additional insights about the model's performance. Your expert input is crucial for us to refine and improve the AI's diagnostic capabilities. We greatly appreciate your valuable contribution to this significant endeavor.
