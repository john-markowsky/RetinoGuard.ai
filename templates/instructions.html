Welcome to the RetinoGuard AI Survey

Understanding Your Role

This interface is designed to allow experts like you to evaluate retinal images and provide a critical assessment of our AI model's predictions.

Instructions
  1. Initial Analysis:
    • Analyze the provided retinal image (1ST Image).
    • Select the appropriate DR rating from the drop-down menu.
    • Submit your rating by clicking 'Submit Rating'.
  2. Model Prediction Review:
    • Upon submission, the AI model's prediction will be displayed (2ND Image).
    • Compare the model's prediction to your initial analysis.
    • Select an accuracy score for the visual diagnosis and disease severity from the drop-down menus.
    • Submit your feedback by clicking 'Submit Feedback'.
What to Expect Next

Once you submit your ratings, you'll receive immediate feedback on the model's performance. Thank you for your valuable contribution to improving AI diagnostics in healthcare.
